# Space Dust Field

Use for Active Theory-style scenes with cosmic dust, nebula color, and particle depth around a central object.

## Shape

- Use at least two layers: distant sparse stars and central colored dust around the hero object.
- For a spine/card spiral, place dust in loose helical arms around the vertical axis.
- Add a few soft nebula planes, but keep them blurred and low opacity.
- Keep constellation lines rare and subtle; too many lines look like UI wireframes.

## Color

- Palette: cyan, blue, violet, magenta, green, small warm specks.
- Avoid uniform confetti. Mix particle sizes and opacity.

## QA

- Check multiple scroll/camera stops because particles can look good in one view and noisy in another.
